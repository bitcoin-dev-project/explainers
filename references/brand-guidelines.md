## Typography
- **Quicksand** — Regular (body text, paragraphs)
- **Montserrat** — Bold, uppercase (headlines); Bold, lowercase (subheadlines); Regular is NOT used for headlines

### Font Usage
- Headlines (uppercase): Montserrat Bold
- Headlines (lowercase): Montserrat Bold
- Paragraph text: Quicksand Regular
- Links, labels, captions, tags: Quicksand Regular

## Characters
Hedgehog mascots with colored outfits. Each character is distinguishable by clothing color.
- Alice, Bob, Carol, Eve/Mallory — each gets a unique outfit color
- Characters have expressive poses and reactions
- Evolved from earlier stick-figure style to illustrated hedgehogs
